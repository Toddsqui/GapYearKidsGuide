About Us

The Gap Year Kids' brand is a consultation service to help high school and college graduates connect with recruiters and services to experience life overseas.

Whether it's working for the Peace Corps, a WOOF organization, or teach English at one of the many public and private outlets around South East Asia, there is an opportunity for most graduates abroad to expand their breadth of knowledge and expertise through teaching others. Hone your communication skills, learn to lead a team of students deferring to you for instruction, and become an effective manager. There are is so much to learn as a teacher, and we hope you'll choose us to get you started in your journey!

Come build memories with us!

Skills & Experience

Teaching Solutions

We provide you with teaching strategies to help ease the transition into teaching large groups of students as well as students with a critical need to learn the target language. We provide our experience and activities and exercises for you to initiate in your classes to get the students improving fast!

Adjusting Overseas

We have traveled extensively and know the ins and outs of many of the countries that you are interested in traveling to. Would you like to live on the kibbutz? Teach in Laos or Vietnam? We provide you with strategies for adjusting to life in another country where the standards of living may be greatly different from your own.
